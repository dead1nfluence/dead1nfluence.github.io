> NTLM Relay Beyond SMB Signing

Posted: April 23, 2025 | Author: dead1nfluence

SMB signing is enabled across the domain? That’s not the end. In this post, we look at alternate services, fallback channels, and clever coercion paths that keep the relay alive.

[~] Alternate Targets

Services like LDAP, RPC, and Exchange expose relay paths, even with signing on. Particularly, if LDAP signing is disabled or LDAPS allows weak channel binding settings.

[~] Example Responder Config

[Responder]
SMB = Off
HTTP = On
LDAP = On

[~] Real-World Payload Drop

Here’s a command used in a live engagement:

impacket-ntlmrelayx -t ldap://10.10.0.5 --delegate-access --escalate-user jsmith
